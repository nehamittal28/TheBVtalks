M.Tech. (Master of Technology)

Computer Science

M. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering is a two year programme that provides the student the ability to understand fundamental principles of science and technology, solve problems and to continuously learn multidisciplinary concepts through MTech CSE Syllabus.

Information Technology

MTech Information Technology is a 2-year post-graduate degree program that aims to provide both theoretical and practical knowledge on the advanced fundamental study of computer-based information systems.

Electronics and Electrical Engineering

M.Tech. Electrical Engineering involves subjects of study such as Control Systems, Circuits and Micro-controllers, Power Engineering, Electronics, Power Systems, Industrial Engineering, Industrial Electrical Systems etc. Student can either opt to work in an industry or research-based career options.

Electronics and Communication

Master of Technology in Electronics is a Postgraduate Electronic Engineering Program which aims to deal with the study and application of electrons and electricity. Engineering in Electronics deals with the study of electronics, electricity and electromagnetism.

Electronics and Instrumentation

Master of technology (MTech) in electronics and instrumentation is a 2-year post graduation degree. MTech electronics and instrumentation engineering deals with the theoretical and practical training in the operation and the design and maintenance of electronic instruments and other digital logical systems.

Mechatronics

MTech Mechatronics or Master of Technology Mechatronics is a postgraduate engineering degree course of 2 years. The subjects taught include topics such as Industrial Management, Mechatronic Systems and its applications, Wireless Communications, Control systems, Signal processing etc.

Biotechnology

MTech (Biotechnology) or Master of Technology in Biotechnology is a two-year postgraduate degree program offered in the field of modern medical practice that revolves around the study of living organisms and bioprocesses.

Chemical Engineering

Master of Technology or MTech Civil Engineering is a 2 year postgraduate technical program which deals with designing, supervision and building of roads, bridges, tunnels, buildings, airports, dams, sewage systems, ports etc.

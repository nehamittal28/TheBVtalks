MSc Electronics is a two years postgraduate degree programme that covers the area of studies like Electronics, digital and analogue signal, semiconductors, IC etc.

Physics

M.Sc in Physics is the course where students get the knowledge about the work of research, that is, master in the field of Science. After pursuing the course of M.Sc in Physics, the candidates can make a career in the field of research scientist, lab manager, data scientist, and many more.

Chemistry

MSC chemistry is a course that includes organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, pharmaceutical chemistry, and analytical chemistry.

Geology

MSc Geology is a postgraduate degree course of 2- year duration, divided into four semesters. This course basically teaches students about the surrounding environment and focuses on the scientific study of the workings and design of the earth and its layers.

Geographyh

MSc in Geography is a degree program that studies the branch of natural science that analyzes patterns in Earth's atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere, and geosphere. Students examine both historic and recent changes in the natural environment.

Enviromental Science

M.Sc. Environmental Science is a two-year postgraduate programme divided into 4 semesters. The program aims to address the growing need for competent professionals in society for applying most effective management practices drawn from various disciplines to create innovative solutions for a sustainable future.

Home Science

MSc Home Science is commonly offered as a 2-year postgraduate program and encompasses the study of home economics, family ecosystems, social institutions, hygiene, nutrition, child development, community living, to name a few.

BioInformatics

Biological sciences are more data driven than ever before and bioinformatics is now central to modern biological research. This programme will teach you how to analyse, integrate, visualise and manipulate large datasets, make new discoveries and reveal new insights in biological sciences.

B.Tech. (Bachelor of Technology)

Computer Science

B.Tech. Computer Science aims to provide students with intricate knowledge of computer technologies and functional operations along with programming, coding, Web and database development.

Information Technology

B.Tech. in Information Technology is a program with a specialization in the field of Information and Technology. The duration of this degree course is generally 4 years, extendable up to 6 years in exceptional cases.

Electronics and Electrical Engineering

B.Tech. in EEE i is a field of engineering that generally deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism.

Electronics and Communication

B.Tech. ECE is program focused on technologies in critical areas such as telecommunications, electricity and electronics, while promoting the development of the skills required to combine hardware and software components.

Electronics and Instrumentation

B.Tech. in EIE is a four-year undergraduate engineering course that deals with the study of designing instrumentations, production of instrumentations, measurements, calibration, quality assurance, etc.

Mechatronics

B.Tech. in Mechatronics is a diverse branch of engineering covering various engineering fields such as mechanics, electronics, computers and controls. The extensive syllabus is designed to help the candidates understand how these branches can be used to achieve efficient production and manufacturing.

Biotechnology

B.Tech. Biotechnology is programme that involves Chemical Engineering Principles and Applied Biology that considers the use of living things in Engineering, Technology, Medicine and other useful applications.

Chemical Engineering

B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering is the study of the principles and laws of physics, chemistry, mathematics, biology, and economics, to the mechanism of converting chemicals or raw components into more useful or valuable forms.

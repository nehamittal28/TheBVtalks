B.Sc.

BioTechnology

Biotechnology combines engineering technologies with biological sciences to regulate systems and living organisms. This undergraduate degree program aims to teach advanced lessons in the biological processes meant for industrial and other purposes.

Mathematics

BSc Mathematics is designed to improve your analytical skills and give you the best understanding of the subject. The degree program covers subjects that are common to other fields like Engineering, Computer Science, Finance, Accounting, and Business Administration.

Computer Science

BSc Computer Science is a program that deals with different topics related to computer science, computer applications, and other related fields. The students can do BSc Computer Science course after completing their class XII examination in PCM stream with a minimum of 50% marks.

Electronics

BSc in electronics is a course that specialises in electrical circuits. It involves the study of electrical components, such as transistors, integrated circuits and diodes.

Physics

B.Sc. in Physics is a program comprising of theory and experimental courses mainly from Physics and few interdisciplinary courses from Mathematics Chemistry and Computer Science.

Chemistry

BSc Chemistry focuses on the study of various branches of chemistry such as Inorganic Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry and Analytical Chemistry along with elective subjects like Analytical methods in Chemistry, Polymer Chemistry and Industrial Chemicals and Environment.

Botany

A bachelor's of science in botany is a programme in plant science, which aims at scientifically studying the life of a plant. This course includes practical as a well theoretical study of plants.

Statistics

A BSc Statistics is a program encompassing the field of Statistics that equips students with problem-solving skills and mathematical comprehension essential for tackling diverse statistical applications.

Zoology

BSc Zoology is a 3-year undergraduate program that entails the advanced study and research in the field of Biological Oceanography, Competitive Physiology, Ecology, Developmental and Cell Biology, Vertebrate and Invertebrate Zoology, Parasitology, etc.

Geology

A Bachelor of Science in Geology is the study focused on equipping students with the knowledge of Earth science. The program includes subjects that are related to the properties of the Earth's surface and celestial bodies, such as meteors.

Geography

BSc Geography is a program with an emphasis on Geography for 3 years. It is the study of the Earth and its many properties, characteristics, people and phenomena, in addition to the map and geographical image-interpretation.

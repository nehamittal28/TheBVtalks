Department of Computer Science
BANASTHALI VIDYAPITH

About

The department of Computer Science runs from the buildings "ICICI Centre for Advanced Studies in Computer Science" in Apaji Institute of Mathematics & Applied Computer Technology and Artech - Dalmia I.T. Centre. Both the centres have world class state of the art infrastructure. The classrooms are large, airy with modern projection & PA systems.

Labs

The laboratories are equipped with the latest state of the art hardware and a large repository of software. Both the buildings are internally networked and are connected to the Campus Network of Banasthali which through optical fibers run across all the academic blocks, libraries and PG hostels.

Library

The departmental library is very rich having hardcopy and electronic access to the complete spectrum of IEEE and ACM journals, select springer, Elsevier and SIAM journals and Inflibnet's e-journal services in addition to ready access to sufficient copies of text and reference books.
